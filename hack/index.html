Welcome to Inclusive Support

Empowering people with disabilities by helping them find the best resources and institutions near them.

Disability Types and Daily Struggles

Visual Impairment

Individuals with visual impairments face challenges like navigating unfamiliar spaces, accessing written information, and using digital platforms.

Hearing Impairment

Deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals often struggle with communication barriers in both personal and professional environments.

Mobility Impairment

People with mobility impairments face difficulties with accessibility in public spaces, transportation, and even employment opportunities.

Learning Disabilities

Individuals with learning disabilities may struggle with educational materials and everyday tasks that require mental processing and concentration.

About Inclusive Support

Inclusive Support is dedicated to helping individuals with disabilities find institutions that meet their unique needs. Our goal is to provide accessible, user-friendly services for all.
